# FP Ireland Phone Script — Brochure Follow-Up

**Goal:** Book a 20-min discovery call. That's it. Don't sell the franchise on this call.

---

## OPENER

> Hi, can I speak with [FNAME]?
>
> Hey [FNAME], it's Prakash from Fully Promoted Ireland. I sent you over the franchise brochure recently, just wanted to make sure it got through and see if anything jumped out at you?

**IF THEY SAY THEY HAVEN'T LOOKED AT IT:**
> No worries at all, I know how it is. Can I give you the 2-minute version?

**IF THEY SAY THEY DID LOOK AT IT:**
> Great! What did you make of it? Any questions come to mind?

---

## BRIEF PITCH (only if they haven't read it or need a refresher)

> So in a nutshell, Fully Promoted is the world's largest promotional products franchise. We've been going for 25 years, about 300 locations in 10 countries.
>
> What franchise owners do is become the go-to marketing resource for businesses in their area. Branded merchandise, uniforms, print, corporate gifts. The nice thing is it's all recurring, businesses come back every quarter.
>
> We're now launching in Ireland and I'm looking for franchise partners.

---

## DISCOVERY QUESTIONS (pick 2-3, keep it conversational)

> Out of curiosity, what was it that got you interested in a franchise in the first place?
>
> What do you do now for work? [Try to connect their background to the business]
>
> Have you looked at other franchises or is this the first one you've explored?
>
> Would you be looking at something full-time or alongside what you're doing now?

---

## HANDLE "DO I NEED EXPERIENCE?"

> Not at all. Most of our successful franchise owners came from completely different backgrounds. There's a 4-week training programme, first two weeks at HQ in Florida where they cover everything, then two weeks with you locally getting you set up. And the support is ongoing after that.

---

## HANDLE "WHAT'S THE INVESTMENT?"

> The total investment is in the range of around EUR 80,000, which includes the franchise fee, full store fit-out, equipment, stock, training, everything. It's completely turnkey, you're ready to trade from day one. That's actually something I can walk you through properly on a follow-up call if you'd like.

---

## HANDLE "IS THERE A MARKET IN IRELAND?"

> Every business in Ireland needs branded products. Think about it, uniforms, corporate gifts, event merchandise, marketing materials. And right now there's no dominant franchise serving that space here. That's the gap.

---

## THE BOOK (this is the only goal)

> Look [FNAME], I think the best next step would be a proper chat where I can walk you through how it all works, territories, the numbers, what your day-to-day would actually look like. It's about 20 minutes.
>
> I can send you a calendar link right now so you can pick a time that suits you. Would that work?

**IF YES:**
> Perfect, I'll text/email it to you right after this call. Looking forward to it.

**IF HESITANT:**
> No pressure at all. Tell you what, I'll send you the link anyway and you can book whenever suits. No rush.

**IF NO / NOT NOW:**
> Completely understand. If things change down the road, you have my number. Best of luck with everything, [FNAME].

---

## AFTER THE CALL

> Great chatting with you, [FNAME]. I'll send that calendar link now. Talk soon.

## CHEAT SHEET — DO's and DON'Ts

| DO | DON'T |
|----|-------|
| Keep it under 5 minutes | Don't read a script robotically |
| Ask questions, let them talk | Don't dump features |
| Mirror their language back | Don't say "business opportunity" |
| Book the next step, that's it | Don't quote exact figures unless asked |
| Be okay with "not now" | Don't pressure or guilt trip |
| Send calendar link immediately after | Don't promise to "call back next week" |
