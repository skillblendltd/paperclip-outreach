Method guide
Screen print · Embroidery DTG · Heat transfer.
1
Screen printing
Cheapest at volume. Setup cost per color. 100+ units.
2
Embroidery
Premium feel. Stitch-count pricing. Durable on polos, caps.
3
Direct-to-garment (DTG)
Full-color, no setup. Best under 50 units or detailed art.
4
Heat transfer / DTF
Fast turnaround, small runs, names and numbers.
