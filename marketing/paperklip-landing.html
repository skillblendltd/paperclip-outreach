How it works

Three steps to a
pipeline that never sleeps

Set it up once. Paperklip handles the daily grind of outbound sales - finding, reaching, and converting prospects while you focus on delivery.

01

Define your audience

Tell Paperklip who your ideal customers are. It scrapes Google Maps, BNI directories, and industry databases to build a qualified prospect list.

02

Launch your campaign

Choose your email sequences, call scripts, and send schedule. Paperklip A/B tests everything and optimizes what works.

03

Check your calendar

Paperklip handles every reply, follows up at the right time, and books meetings directly into your calendar. You just show up.

Capabilities

Everything a sales team does.
None of the headcount.

One platform replaces your SDR, your email tool, your dialer, and your CRM. All working together, all on autopilot.

Multi-sequence email drips

5-touch email sequences with A/B testing, smart timing, and per-campaign rate limits. Personalized at scale using prospect data.

AI-powered voice calls

Automated outbound calls via AI voice agents. Segment-specific scripts that learn from every conversation and improve over time.

Intelligent reply handling

Reads every response, classifies intent, suppresses opt-outs, and generates personalized replies in your voice. Not templates - real conversations.

Built-in lead generation

Scrape Google Maps, BNI directories, and industry databases. Auto-deduplicate and import directly into campaigns.

Multi-tenant architecture

Run multiple products, brands, and personas from one platform. Each with their own voice, templates, and suppression lists.

Pipeline intelligence

Track every prospect from cold to booked. See what's working, what's not, and let the AI optimize your sequences automatically.
