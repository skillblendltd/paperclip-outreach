# TaggIQ Warm Pipeline — Call Scripts

**Date prepared:** 2026-05-05
**Purpose:** Founder-led phone calls to convert warm replies into booked demos / closed customers.
**Use this doc:** Print or have open during calls. Each prospect gets their own block. Tick the tracking row at the bottom as you go.

---

## How to use this doc

- **Open question first, listen for 2 minutes, never pitch in the first 60 seconds.** Every call below opens with a question, not a statement.
- **Record outcome immediately** in the tracking table at the bottom — date called, what they said, next step.
- **Update the prospect record** in Django admin (notes + status) within 1 hour of the call so the pipeline state stays accurate.
- **Never confirm a meeting time on the call** unless you literally have your calendar open and free in front of you. Default to "I'll send a calendar invite when I'm at my desk."
- **No price quotes on the call.** Even if asked. "I'd want to scope properly before pricing." Always.
- **Founding 50 (40% off year 1)** is the closing lever — never lead with it.

- Re-read this prospect's block (60 seconds)

- Have your calendar open so you can offer slots without saying "I'll check and come back"
- Number to dial in your phone, ready

**During each call:**
- Open with a question, not a pitch
- Listen for 2 minutes before saying anything about TaggIQ
- Take notes (paper is fine — clarity beats search)
- Never quote a price
- Never confirm a meeting time unless calendar is in front of you

**After each call (within 1 hour):**
- Update prospect notes in Django admin
- Update prospect status if it changed (interested → engaged → demo_scheduled)
- Send any promised email (calendar invite, link to fix, follow-up document)
- Tick this doc's tracking row
